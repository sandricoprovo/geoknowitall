Do you consider yourself good at geography? If your answer is yes, or if you just want some fun, Geo Know It All is the game for you!

Game Instructions:
This game breaks countries into world regions, so first you'll pick a region of the world to focus on. The score is based on the number of countries in a region multipled by four(4) questions for each country. This means that you can get a total of four(4) points for each country. If you want to know the number of countries in a region, just divide the score denominator by 4 once you select a region. If you don't know the answer, thats okay! Just hit the next button once to see the correct answers, and then hit it again to go to the next country. It's all about learning, so have fun!

The five regions available are: Africa, Americas, Asia, Europe & Oceania.

Select the region you'd like to play from the dropdown menu below.
